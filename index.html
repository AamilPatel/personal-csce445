Challenges I Experience With My Media Tools

  • Moving Images (Live TV) The biggest challenge that I face in the moving images category is with Live TV. Often times when watching sports live the stream tends to buffer or give out entirely. This is not as prominent in the other forms of moving images becaues they are not live and can be downloaded. Along with this, it is often difficult to find software fot live shows nowdays because streaming has become so prevelant. Even when you do find something often times you have to pay for an entirely new subscription.
  • Text & Messaging The biggest challenge I have with texting and messaging is that it is hard to keep up with all the different platforms that I communicate through. For email it is relatively easy because I only have to look through two, my personal and my school. However for messaging, some of my friends use Google Messages or iMessage that requires SMS and some prefer apps like Signal and WhatsApp that can utilize Wifi to message and call. On top of this I also need to message in apps like Slack and GroupMe for school and work. This can become very overwhelming quickly.
